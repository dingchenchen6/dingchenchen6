# Chenchen Ding | СИЂТЎеТЎе

Peking University - IIASA International Joint Postdoctoral Fellow

I am Chenchen Ding (СИЂТЎеТЎе), a biodiversity researcher working on global change biology, conservation biology, biogeography, species distribution, biodiversity change, community structure, ecosystem services, and extinction risk assessment.

My research focuses on understanding and predicting how global change, especially land-use change and climate change, affects biodiversity. I work on species distributions, biodiversity monitoring, ecological modelling, extinction risk assessment, and conservation priority identification.
